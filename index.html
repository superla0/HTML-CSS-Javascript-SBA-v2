Welcome to my Under0ath Fan Site!

This website was constructed to serve as a tool to display my knowledge in html, css, and javascript. This website will hopefully fulfill all the parameters set forth by the SBA assignment and shows that I have a very basic understanding of how all this front end framework stuff works.

This website also pulls from ideas I've seen from other websites as well as my own independent research. The layout and formatting of everything were ideas from other websites I've seen, and the information presented are all sourced from official website and social media accounts affiliated with the band, Under0ath.
